Description

The organized crime boss, Big Al, is living a life full of drugs, money, and firearms, and he’s engaged in a love triangle that takes his organization through the most difficult time to date. Experience challenges its chances and leadership, which can not collapse in the face of dangerous threats, proving that a partnership is a must when it comes to the law of the street code. Nisha, the Big Als girl, is part of the love triangle of a man in her past (another drug boss) who brings issues into her future. The attitude of the different bosses absorbs her passion, overwhelming her perception of the acts that are taking place in her harsh lifestyle, and challenges are coming, creating consequences for what’s right and what’s wrong. Loyalty and cohesion and strength emerge with brain versus violence, while the two gangs totally separate neighbors. Death is to be thought through before dishonor. The book is an action-packed novel that revolves around problems in urban society that emerge in many relationships and communities. Can page is exciting and easy to read and entertain, pulling the reader deeper and deeper into the plot.
